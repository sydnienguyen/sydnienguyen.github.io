Hyaluronic Acid Serum

A page for Hyaluronic Acid Serum. My thoughts on hyaluronic acid is that I really like it. It is an humectant which means that it helps draw moisture from the air into the skin or retain the moisture in the skin to say on the skin. I love to use it as a base before putting on any other serums. I like applying it onto my face when it is still wet. The following are my reviews of products that I have tried.

Naturium’s hyaluronic acid 01/18/21. I like how smooth it is. I don’t like how i have to shake it before using it because sometime i forget. i really like this hyaluronic acid tho bc its a gel texture so its not runny. I like the pump that it is packaged in bc it makes it easy to get the product. plus the product doesn’t crust on the nozzle like other hyaluronic acid serums that ive used. ran out on 3/19/21 so it lasts for almost exactly 2 months with a pump for day and another for night.





The Inkey List’s Hyaluronic Acid Serum

11/8/20 - 01/18/21
Buy again: No
1.0 fl. oz./39 mL for $7.99
Used for AM and PM. $7.99 for 1 oz which is really cheap. It doesn’t smell like anything so thats great. only 2% hyaluronic acid compared to The Ordinary’s 5%. However, on the ingredient lists it is after Phenoxyethanol which is only allowed to have 1% in any product so is this product really 2%? Claims to “hydrate and plump skin”. It great to start now bc it is getting cold and it could be really dry so i make sure that my face is still damp when putting on this serum. I hate the packaging bc it is difficult to squeeze 1 pea size out of that kind of container.
