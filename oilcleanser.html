Oil Cleanser

A page for all my oil cleanser reviews.

Purito’s Green Cleansing Oil

02/28/22-06/10/22
Buy again: Yes Yes Yes
$23.60 for 200 mL./ 6.76 fl. oz
I’ve used this cleansing oil before so this is a repurchase. I like how it doesn’t have fragrance and it was easy to apply on the skin bc it didn’t need time to emulsify. However, it doesn’t take off waterproof mascara. Easy to clean off with water. I would recommend and buy again.

Naturium’s Fermented Camellia Creamy Cleansing Oil

12/06/21- 02/26/22
Buy again: No
103.5 mL/ 3.5 fl. oz for $20.00
It takes some time to work it in but it doesn’t completely feel like it cleans off with just water so a second cleanser is necessary. takes off makeup but makes eyes blurry if too much is used. doesn’t take off waterproof mascara tho. doesn’t have fragrance. wouldn’t necessarily buy again.

Dear Klairs’s Gentle Black Deep Cleansing Oil.

10/16/21 - 12/05/21
Buy again: NO!!!
150 mL for $24.20
it’s not good. It’s so hard to wash off with just water. I have to use a cleanser to wash it off. It does take off my makeup but it doesn’t take off itself. I’m like starting to break out on my forehead and this is the only thing that’s I’ve been trying that is new. I will give it 2 week trial but will definitely stop using if the breakout continues. This doesn’t make me breakout bc my skin went back to being good. Wouldn’t buy again tho bc its too hard to wash off and messy.
