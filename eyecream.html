Eye Cream

A page for eye cream. my thoughts on eye cream is that it is not necessary if you have a good moisturizer. However, it is a nice luxury. Be gentle around the eyes because the skin around the eyes is the most thin and sensitive part of your body.

The Inkey List’s Caffeine Eye Cream

01/30/2021 1st
08/19/2021- 10/15/2021 2nd
Buy again? Yes
0.5 oz for $9.99
Started 1/30/21. I started using in the PM because i use their Bright-i in the morning. started 2nd 08/19/21-10/25/21. It’s a gel light consistency. Doesnt have fragrance but idk if it does anything bc I can’t tell if it makes my dark circles go away.

The Inkey List Brighten-i Eye Cream

11/17/2020 - 08/19/2021
Buy again? Yes
0.5 oz for $9.99
It has an applicator end which is great especially when I put it in the fridge and it gets cold. Its a gel consistency which I like. I like how Inkey List tries to educate their consumers about their products bc they have great description on what’s in the product and when the use the product. It’s meant to be used within 6 months but I only used it in the AM which made it last for a long time

Glow Recipe’s Avocado Melt Retinol Eye Sleeping Mask (sample).

07/29/2020 - 08/17/2020
Buy again? No
Maybe since it’s been sitting in my collection for 2 months but I noticed that it shrunk. It is a VERY THICK consistency so it was difficult to apply. First impression: fine lines look like they have flattened out which is very surprising. I think it really works bc the fine lines under and around my eyes went away.

bareMinerals’s SkinLongevity Vital Power Eye Gel Cream

08/18/2020 - 11/16/2020
Buy again? No

Belif’s moisturizing eye bomb (sample)

Innisfree’s Green Tea Seed Intensive Hydrating Eye Cream

08/2019 - 05/2020
