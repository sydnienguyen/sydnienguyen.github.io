Cleanser

A page for all my cleanser reviews. I like cleansers that are gel like, that do not have fragrance, cleanse skin but doesn't strip. Foam is okay but not too much.

Pyunkang Yul’s Low pH Pore Deep Cleansing Foam.

01/31/22- 04/11/22. 04/17/22- Current
Buy again: No
100ml or 3.4 fl. oz. $10.20.
It has a raspberry scent. It’s a light cleanser that foams up. It’s ok. I wouldn’t buy again. It has a terrible formulation because the cleanser separates from each other so I have to shake to mix it back together every time I use it which was not good. One part water and other part the little cleansing beads.

Dam Dam’s Nomad’s Cream Purifying Cleanser (sample).

04/11/22- 04/16/22.
Buy again: No
It has like exfoliating beads that I didn’t like. It didn’t really foam up a lot. I wouldn’t buy it.

Youth to the People’s Kale+Green Tea Spinach Vitamins Superfood Cleanser (sample)

03/13/22-03/14/22
It smelled like green apple. Skincare community raves about this cleanser but it was mid to me. Wouldn’t buy.

B.Lab’s Matcha Hydrating Foam Cleanser

10/31/21- 02/20/22.
Buy again? Maybe
4.05 fl oz for $9.60
I like this cleanser. It smells like matcha and in a good way. I just dont know how it isn’t a pump so kinda inconvenient to open and squeeze if my hands are wet. It’s a very thick cleanser so its hard to squeeze. It foams but not a lot which I like. I would buy again. It makes my face clean and gentle.

CosRx salicylic acid cleanser

04/19/2021- 11/02/2021
Buy again? No
5.07 fl oz for $13.86
Has a tea tree oil smell that doesn’t bother me. It’s a creamy cleanser that is quite thick compared to gel cleansers that I’ve used previously

Glossier’s Milk Jelly Cleanser

02/2020 - 08/2020
Buy again? 8/10 no
$18
GEL consistency. It does not foam up. It smells like roses. I first used the sample and thought it was very plain but it was very gentle which was something different that I’ve never used before. I like that it is like a jelly. I also like that I can put it on dry skin and it will melt/breakdown the makeup. Feels gentle on my skin. Glossier’s Milk Jelly Cleanser. I like this cleanser bc it’s a gel and it doesn’t foam up. Super gentle on my skin which I like. Only thing I don’t like is that it kinda smells like roses but I got used to it. The bottle lasted about 6 months for $18 for 6 oz. I like the applicator that it comes with bc it’s the perfect amount to wash my face with. It takes off makeup very gently which I love. 2/20-8/8/20. Would definitely recommend for a gentle cleanse but also cleans makeup and oil.
