A page for all my toner reviews.

Purito’s Centella Unscented Toner (sample)

01/10/22 - 01/11/22
No but maybe
it really is unscented. Doesn’t sting my face. Claims to soothe and hydrate. I think it’s like another form of hyaluronic acid but idk. Honestly nice but idk what toner does so its not worth buying.

Ren’s Ready Steady Glow AHA Lotion Tonique

8/9/2020 - idk when
No
$52 for 16.9 oz.
First impression is that it looks like it exfoliates a lot more dirt off my skin like the cotton pad is browner than usual and I’ve been using the same cleanser. I think it changed me skin a lot. Its a lot smoother and brighter. Smells slightly citrusy but I had to try to smell it to smell the scent or I couldn’t tell at all.
