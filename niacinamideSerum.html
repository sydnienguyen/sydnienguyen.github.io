Niacinamide

A page for Niacinamide. my thoughts on Niacinamide is that it is supposed to control oil production. As someone who has oily skin, this ingredient seemed really appealing. Later down the road, I didn't find it very necessary because a lot of my skincare products already had this ingredient so I didn't need to buy one product for this one ingredient.
